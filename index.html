Mini Crossword Game

How to Play

Fill in the crossword puzzle by typing letters into the squares. The puzzle consists of across and down clues.

Controls:

  • Type letters: Enter letters into the highlighted squares.
  • Enter: Switch between across and down directions.
  • Arrow keys: Navigate to adjacent squares.
  • Backspace: Delete the current letter and move to the previous square if empty.
  • Click: Click on a square to focus it.

The current row or column is highlighted in yellow. Use the "Check Answers" button to verify your solution.
